About Our Programs

Welcome to Gardner College! We are thrilled that you are considering joining our vibrant academic community. At Gardner College, we offer cutting-edge undergraduate programs designed to prepare you for a successful career in the ever-evolving world of technology. Our programs are crafted to provide you with a robust foundation, practical skills, and innovative thinking essential for the digital age. Here’s a closer look at the programs we offer:

Courses:

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS)

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE)

Our Bachelor’s programs provide an in-depth education tailored to different aspects of technology: the BSIT program emphasizes practical skills through courses in Network Administration, Database Management, and Cybersecurity; the BSCS curriculum dives into the fundamentals of computing with courses in Algorithms, Programming Languages, and Software Engineering; the BSIS track integrates technology and business with studies in Enterprise Systems, Project Management, and Business Analytics; while the BSCpE degree combines elements of electrical engineering and computer science, featuring coursework in Microprocessor Design, Embedded Systems, and Hardware-Software Integration.
